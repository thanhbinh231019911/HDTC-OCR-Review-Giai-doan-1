IX. NGUYEN TAC COT LOI BAT BUOC DOC TRUOC KHI SUA CODE
------------------------------------------------------

1. Ghep mat truoc/mat sau CCCD, Can cuoc

- Mat truoc va mat sau CCCD/Can cuoc chi duoc lien ket bang day so CCCD/Can cuoc co tren ca 2 mat hoac bang chung ro rang cung mot nguoi trong cung nhom upload.
- Khong ghep lan lon giua Ben bao dam va Ben duoc bao dam.
- Khong ghep cheo giua nguoi nay voi nguoi khac.
- Khong tim bua, khong lay "gan nhat" neu khong co bang chung lien ket bang so CCCD/Can cuoc.
- Khi can lay ngay cap tu mat sau, phai dam bao mat sau do thuoc cung so CCCD/Can cuoc/cung nguoi; neu khong chac thi de trong hoac canh bao de sua tay.
- Voi mat sau CCCD co MRZ dong dau dang "IDVNM...", bo qua phan chu cai va ky tu "<", chi tinh phan so. Neu sau IDVNM co 22 so lien ke thi 10 so dau la thong tin khac, 12 so tiep theo la so CCCD/Can cuoc dung de khop voi mat truoc. Vi du "IDVNM0760027248001076002724<9" cho ra so CCCD "001076002724".
- Quy tac MRZ CCCD nay ap dung chung cho moi nguoi trong ho so, khong phan biet Ben bao dam/Ben duoc bao dam; neu co 4-5 Ben bao dam thi van khop tung nguoi bang so CCCD/Can cuoc rieng, khong dung vi tri hay thu tu gan nhat.
- MRZ chi dung de khop dung nguoi; ngay cap phai doc tu vung mat sau, gan nhan ngay cap hop le. CCCD mau cu chap nhan dong "Ngay, thang, nam / Date, month, year"; Can cuoc mau moi chap nhan dong "Ngay, thang, nam cap / Date of issue".
- Ngay cap chi duoc lay tren cung dong voi nhan ngay cap hoac toi da 2 dong ke tiep. Chap nhan cac dinh dang dd/MM/yyyy, dd-MM-yyyy, dd.MM.yyyy va ddMMyyyy khi OCR lam mat dau "/".
- Chi sua loi OCR nhe trong vung ngay cap hop le, vi du O/o/Q/D thanh 0, I/l/T thanh 1, r thanh 7, va bo 1 ky tu thua neu chuoi ngay dang ddMM?yyyy. Sau khi sua bat buoc validate ngay/thang/nam hop le. Vi du OCR "Oro712021" ngay sau nhan "Ngay, thang, nam / Date, month, year" duoc doc thanh "07/07/2021".
- Neu khong co nhan ngay cap hop le, khong khop dung mat sau theo so CCCD/Can cuoc, co nhieu ngay ung vien khac nhau, hoac ngay sau sua khong hop le thi de trong/canh bao sua tay; khong lay ngay gan nhat.

2. Review thong tin tai san bat dong san

- Man hinh review tai san phai the hien dung noi dung tren bia dat/giay chung nhan, bao gom dau cham, dau phay, dau ngoac, dau muc I, II, 1, 2, a), b), c)... theo dung thu tu va cach ghi trong bia dat.
- Khi xuat hop dong, mau Word chi la khung trinh bay; phan mo ta tai san phai lay theo cac truong thuc te doc duoc tren bia dat/giay chung nhan. Co truong nao thi ghi truong do, khong duoc bo qua chi vi mau khong co, va khong duoc them noi dung mau neu tren bia dat khong co.
- Nguon goc su dung dat, ghi chu/thay doi sau cap, tai san gan lien voi dat chi hien thi trong hop dong khi bia dat co noi dung thuc. Cac tieu de bang, chu ky, so vao so, dong "Chua chung nhan quyen so huu", de trong, "-/-" hoac tuong tu khong duoc coi la noi dung mo ta thuc.
- Khi OCR bia dat co bo cuc khac thuong hoac mot muc bi xuong dong, phai uu tien tach theo chi muc muc/diem: II, 1, 2, a), b), c), d), đ), e), g). Noi dung cua mot muc keo dai den ngay truoc chi muc ke tiep. Vi du `b) Dia chi` phai lay ca dong tiep theo cho den truoc `c) Dien tich`, khong chi lay dong cuoi.
- Khong tu dien giai lai, khong rut gon, khong doi cach viet neu OCR/bia dat da co noi dung ro.
- Vi du dien tich co dong bang chu thi phai giu dung dang: "88,7 m2 (Bang chu: Tam muoi tam phay bay met vuong)", khong duoc hien thanh "88,7 m2 Tam muoi tam phay bay met vuong".
- Cac hang muc ve nha o, cong trinh xay dung, tai san khac gan lien voi dat neu bia dat ghi "Chua chung nhan quyen so huu", de trong, "-/-" hoac gia tri tuong tu thi khong can hien thi noi dung chi tiet ben duoi, neu co.
- Neu khong doc chac thong tin tren bia dat thi uu tien de trong/canh bao/sua tay, khong tu suy dien.
